I am a Master of Data Science student at Monash University in my last year, passionate about analytics and business transformation. I have been studying intensively for the past four years to become a Data Scientist, and I am always eager to learn more.

I am currently developing a master thesis under the supervision of Dr Christoph Bergmeir. The project is co-supervised by researchers from the Orygen Institute. Our purpose is to make use of Recurrent Neural Networks to forecast missing values in Medical time-series datasets.

I have a second background in Business and Economics, and I have interned in Financial Markets firms and worked as a Treasury Financial Analyst in the largest Brazilian retail company. My goal as a professional is to make use of the range of skills developed in my career and education to help companies transition to the new economy.

In my free time, I like to be in Nature, study Logosophy and practice sports. Nothing makes me happier than a good swim in a beautiful waterfall. I am also very much interested in politics in general and spend a lot of time wondering how humanity will look like 50 years from now (I hope much better).
